About Us

The Front-End Developer Roadmap is a website that outlines the steps to becoming a front-end developer. It provides a foundation for each topic and explains the syntax of several computer languages, including as HTML, CSS, and Javascript. It also offers a concept of how to be able to study the front-end development in a step by step process. This website will assist with the development of the client-side of an online application, which is the visual portion of a web application that a user interacts with when it is launched. There are also recommendations about which program to use for building and testing applications.

VISION

Our website has the vision:

  • to encourage more people in development of their own applications with the use of this roadmap.
  • to see more new inovation when it comes to front-end designs.
  • to help beginner programmers to start their journey in developing applications.
  • to enhace the passion from current front-end developers.

MISSION

This website mission is to provide developers with quality knowledge and information that is needed in front-end development. The website provide neccessary tools and learnings through a step by step roadmap that will serve as a strategic plan and communicaiton tool that guides the developer to be able to reach their goals.
